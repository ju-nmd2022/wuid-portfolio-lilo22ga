Things to do

Though I haven't lived here for very long yet, I still have a couple of tips on some very effortless and calm activities to do here in Jönköping.

  • go on walks (there's so many nice trails here!)
  • explore your surroundings on bike
  • go to secondhands

Things to eat

I can't say that I have that much knowledge about places to go eat either, life as a student.. BUT when I was at AKA last week, I had the vegetarian nuggets with garlic sauce, and they were *chefs kiss*. Only costs like 40 kronor too, luvs it!

Things to visit

Some recommendations on places you could go to.

  • Erikshjälpen secondhand (all of them are quite good)
  • Vattenledningsparken, very nice view of the city!
  • Torghandeln Västra torget, market that's open every saturday morning
  • Lindströms Cafe & Konditori, cheap coffe :)
