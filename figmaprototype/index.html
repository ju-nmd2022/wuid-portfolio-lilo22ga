Figma prototype

In this project us students were supposed to design a prototype for a streaming app. I chose to create a podcast app. As you can see I ended up with a darker color scheme, and quite calm colors aswell to make the layout easy on the eyes, but also to not take away attention from the nr 1 star here - the different podcasts! Reappearing in this design is also the usage of lines (and background boxes), this to emphasize where one section ends and another begins, all in all to make the whole thing easier to navigate through. The basic structure is inspired by similar streaming services, but it finds its own style through typeface, colors and details :)
